About This Training

Course Overview

Title: AI and Generative AI for School Inspectors in Portugal
Author & Trainer: François Jourde
Version: 1.8
Last Updated: 7 February 2026
Level: Intermediate

Description

This 3-day training develops Portuguese school inspectors' digital competences, enabling them to work confidently, responsibly, and efficiently with AI, including generative AI, in their professional practice. The goal is to transform AI from an abstract concept into a tangible tool that solves work-related friction.

Note: The trainer does not use PowerPoint presentations. Instead, all participants receive this HTML-based single-page web application that can be opened in any browser and updated in real-time.

Target Audience

General Inspectorate of Education and Science (IGEC): Portuguese school inspectors working across primary and secondary education cycles (English as a foreign language).

Participants have varying digital competences and need to develop their skills to work responsibly and efficiently with AI, including generative AI.

Cohort Size: 30 participants

Prerequisites

  • Basic familiarity with Microsoft Office suite
  • Access credentials for Microsoft Copilot (distributed in advance)
  • Basic level of literacy sufficient to decode visual, textual and audio information

Duration & Structure

Total Duration: 18 hours (3 days)
Designed Duration: 17.3 hours
Day 1: Understanding GenAI
Foundations, Governance, Legal, Ethics and competences
Duration: 6 hours
Day 2: Practical Application & Prompting
Hands-on practice with AI tools and prompting techniques
Duration: 6 hours
Day 3: Building Shared Prompt Libraries
Equipping the inspectorate with shared resources
Duration: 6 hours

Learning Approach

Modality: Blended Learning

The training combines theoretical foundations with hands-on practice, encouraging active participation and collaborative learning. Participants progress from understanding AI concepts to applying them in their professional context and creating shared resources for their organization.

Tools & Materials

LMS GenAI EdTech Web Tools
  • Microsoft Teams (Learning Management)
  • Microsoft Copilot (Generative AI)
  • Microsoft Word (Word Processing)
  • Wooclap (Audience Response System)
  • Transformer Explainer (Web Simulation)
  • Prompting Coach (ChatBot)
